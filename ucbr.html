UC Browser

UC Browser is a popular, fast, and feature-rich mobile web browser developed by Alibaba, widely used for its data-saving technology, video acceleration, and high-speed downloading capabilities. It is designed for low-end devices and slow network environments, featuring a built-in ad blocker and, more recently, a built-in VPN for improved privacy.
