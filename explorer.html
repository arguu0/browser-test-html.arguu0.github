Internet Explorer (discontinued)

Internet Explorer (IE) was a dominant Microsoft-developed web browser launched in 1995, widely used until the early 2010s. It was discontinued, with support officially ending on June 15, 2022, for most Windows 10 versions, replaced by Microsoft Edge. IE was known for ActiveX technology and, later, its security, performance, and web standard limitations.
