DuckDuckGo Browser

The DuckDuckGo browser is a free, privacy-focused web browser available for Windows, Mac, iOS, and Android that automatically blocks trackers, encrypts connections, and provides private searching by default. It offers a "Fire Button" to instantly clear browsing data, does not store personal history, and makes money through private search ads.
